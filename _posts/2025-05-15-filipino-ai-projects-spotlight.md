Filipino AI talent is building real solutions for real Filipino problems. Here's a roundup of five projects that caught our attention.

---

## 1. Filipino Language Corpus & NLP Toolkit

A collaborative, open-source effort to build the largest annotated corpus of Filipino text — spanning Tagalog, Cebuano, Ilocano, Hiligaynon, and more. The project also provides NLP tools (tokenizers, POS taggers, sentiment analyzers) optimized for Philippine languages.

**Why it matters:** Most AI language tools are English-first. This project puts Filipino languages on the map for the global NLP community.

---

## 2. Panahon AI — Weather Forecasting for Farmers

A machine learning model trained on Philippine weather data to provide hyperlocal, 7-day weather forecasts for farming communities. Accessible via SMS for areas with limited internet.

**Why it matters:** Climate variability is one of the biggest threats to Filipino farmers. Better forecasts mean better planting decisions.

---

## 3. Likas — Disaster Response Coordination

An AI-powered platform that aggregates social media, news, and official reports during typhoons and floods to generate real-time situation reports for LGUs and disaster response teams.

**Why it matters:** The Philippines is one of the world's most disaster-prone countries. Faster, better information saves lives.

---

## 4. BahasaAI — Code-Switching Detection

A research project studying and modeling "Taglish" — the natural mix of Tagalog and English in Filipino speech and text. The model helps AI systems understand how Filipinos actually communicate.

**Why it matters:** Most AI models struggle with code-switching. This research makes AI more natural and useful for Filipino users.

---

## 5. MediSalik — AI Health Navigator

A chatbot trained on Philippine health guidelines that helps users navigate health concerns, understand symptoms, and find appropriate health facilities — in Filipino and regional languages.

**Why it matters:** Healthcare access is uneven across the Philippines. AI-assisted health navigation can bridge that gap.
