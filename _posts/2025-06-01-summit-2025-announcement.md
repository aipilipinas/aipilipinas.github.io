We're thrilled to announce that the **AI Pilipinas Summit 2025** is happening on **August 16, 2025** in Manila!

## What to Expect

The Summit is our largest event of the year — a full-day gathering of Filipino AI practitioners, researchers, students, and enthusiasts. This year's theme: **"AI for the Filipino Future."**

### Program Highlights

- **Keynote sessions** from leading Filipino AI researchers and practitioners
- **Panel discussions** on AI policy, responsible AI, and AI in Philippine industries
- **Hands-on workshops** on the latest tools and frameworks
- **Community showcase** — Filipino-built AI projects and startups
- **Networking** — connect with thousands of community members

### Who Should Attend?

Everyone! The Summit is open to all — from AI beginners to seasoned experts. Whether you're a student, developer, researcher, entrepreneur, educator, or just curious about AI, there's something for you.
