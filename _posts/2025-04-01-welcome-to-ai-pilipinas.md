**Mabuhay, and welcome to AI Pilipinas!**

This is the beginning of something we've been building for a long time — a home on the web for the Filipino AI community.

## Who We Are

AI Pilipinas is a community of Filipino practitioners, researchers, students, and enthusiasts united by a belief: that AI should work for Filipinos, and that Filipinos should help shape how AI evolves globally.

We started as a group chat. We've grown into a nationwide network. And now we have a proper home.

## What We're Building

This site is the hub for everything AI Pilipinas:

- **Programs and learning tracks** — free resources to help Filipinos level up in AI
- **Events** — from intimate local meetups to the annual AI Pilipinas Summit
- **News and insights** — AI developments through a Filipino lens
- **Community** — how to connect with fellow Filipino AI enthusiasts wherever you are

## How to Get Involved

1. **Join the community** — [Facebook Group](https://facebook.com/groups/aipilipinas) or [Discord](https://discord.gg/aipilipinas)
2. **Follow us** for updates — [Facebook](https://facebook.com/aipilipinas), [Twitter/X](https://twitter.com/aipilipinas), [LinkedIn](https://linkedin.com/company/aipilipinas)
3. **Attend an event** — check our [Events page](/events/)
4. **Explore programs** — see what's coming on our [Programs page](/programs/)
5. **Share this site** with a Filipino who should know about us
