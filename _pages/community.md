## Volunteer & Get Involved

AI Pilipinas runs entirely on the energy of its volunteers. Here's how you can contribute:

- **Event organizer** — help plan and run meetups or the annual summit
- **Facilitator / trainer** — co-facilitate workshops and bootcamps
- **Content creator** — write for our blog, create explainer videos, or translate resources into Filipino
- **Mentor** — guide students and career-changers into AI
- **Chapter lead** — run your regional chapter

## Community Guidelines

AI Pilipinas is a respectful, inclusive, and constructive space. All members are expected to uphold our community guidelines:

1. Be kind and respectful — no harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
2. Stay on topic — keep discussions relevant to AI, data science, and related fields
3. Share, don't gatekeep — help others learn; credit sources
4. No spam or unsolicited promotion
5. Disagree constructively — debate ideas, not people

Full guidelines are posted in each community channel.
