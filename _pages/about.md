## Our Mission

AI Pilipinas exists to **empower Filipinos through Artificial Intelligence**. We believe that the future of AI in the Philippines must be shaped by Filipinos — not just adopted from abroad. Our mission is to make AI knowledge accessible to every Filipino, regardless of background, location, or resources.

## Our Story

AI Pilipinas was founded by a small group of Filipino AI practitioners who saw a gap: world-class Filipino talent with limited access to local community, mentorship, and learning resources. What started as a group chat has grown into a nationwide network of thousands of members — students, engineers, researchers, educators, and curious minds united by a love for AI and a pride in being Filipino.

## What We Do

### Community Building
We maintain one of the most active AI communities in Southeast Asia — online and offline. From Luzon to Mindanao, our regional chapters bring AI conversations closer to home.

### Education & Upskilling
We run free and low-cost workshops, bootcamps, and learning tracks covering:
- AI & Machine Learning fundamentals
- Natural Language Processing (especially for Filipino languages)
- Computer Vision
- Responsible & Ethical AI
- AI for social good

### Research & Advocacy
We support Filipino AI research, publish accessible explainers on AI policy, and advocate for AI governance that reflects Filipino values and needs.

### Industry Connections
We connect Filipino AI talent with opportunities in local and global tech companies, startups, and research institutions.

## Our Values

| Value | What it means for us |
|---|---|
| **Accessibility** | AI knowledge should be free and available to all Filipinos |
| **Inclusivity** | We welcome everyone — beginners, experts, all regions, all backgrounds |
| **Filipino-first** | We celebrate and amplify Filipino contributions to AI |
| **Responsibility** | We advocate for ethical, fair, and transparent AI |
| **Bayanihan** | We help each other grow — the community is stronger than any individual |
