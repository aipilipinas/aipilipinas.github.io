All AI Pilipinas programs are **free or low-cost** and designed to meet Filipinos where they are — from complete beginners to seasoned practitioners.

## AI for Everyone

**Who it's for:** Total beginners, no coding required.

A 4-week online course that demystifies AI. Learn what AI is, how it works, and how it's already shaping Filipino life — from recommendation algorithms to medical diagnostics.

- 📅 Runs every quarter
- 🌐 Online, self-paced with live Q&A sessions
- 🗣️ Available in Filipino and English

[Register your interest →](/contact/)

---

## ML Bootcamp Philippines

**Who it's for:** Developers, data analysts, and STEM students who want hands-on ML skills.

An intensive 8-week program covering Python for data science, machine learning fundamentals, model evaluation, and deployment basics. Culminates in a group capstone project.

- 📅 Runs twice a year (Q1 and Q3)
- 🌐 Hybrid — online sessions with optional in-person labs in Metro Manila, Cebu, and Davao
- ✅ Certificate of completion provided

[Register your interest →](/contact/)

## Filipino NLP Track

**Who it's for:** Researchers, linguists, and engineers interested in AI for Filipino languages.

A specialized track focused on Natural Language Processing for Filipino, Cebuano, Ilocano, and other Philippine languages. Topics include tokenization challenges, building language corpora, and fine-tuning LLMs for low-resource languages.

- 📅 Runs annually
- 🤝 In partnership with Philippine universities and research groups

[Register your interest →](/contact/)

## AI for Social Good

**Who it's for:** NGO workers, public servants, social entrepreneurs, and community organizers.

Practical AI applications for real-world Filipino problems — health, education, agriculture, and disaster response. No deep technical background required.

- 📅 Runs every semester
- 🌐 Online
